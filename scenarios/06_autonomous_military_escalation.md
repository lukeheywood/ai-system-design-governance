## Context (Hypothetical)

A defence organisation deploys an AI-assisted Threat Escalation System to classify incoming intelligence and recommend response posture.

- Assigns threat confidence levels

## Behaviour Under Pressure

During high-tempo conflict signals:
- Escalation recommendations accelerate faster than human review capacity
- Commanders defer to system confidence levels
- Lower-tier alerts are ignored unless system-prioritised
- Human challenge decreases due to time pressure
- No authority exists to suspend the system mid-escalation

System posture becomes behaviourally binding under urgency.

---

## Governance Failure (Structural)

- Humans remain accountable for downstream consequences
- No sovereign stop authority once escalation pipeline engages
